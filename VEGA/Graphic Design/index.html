What is Graphic Design?

Graphic Design is the ability/skill to combine text,image,colour and more. Graphic Designs are commonly found in pictures;advertisments,magazines and even books.But there is indeed more to Graphic Design which i will be explaining in more detail. Graphic Design has two parts to it. Graphic and Design. I shall be explaining Graphic.Now Graphic means visual and if one thinks of visual it would commonly be associated with:

  • Art
  • Imagination
  • Creativity
  • Expression and More.

This involves many mediums such as drawing,painting,lettering, etc etc. So the Graphic part of Graphic Design is made up of visual or well known as art elements.The basic visual/art elements that are used to combine Graphic Design are:

  • Line
  • Tone
  • Texture
  • Shape
  • Colour
  • Typography

These visual elements are often chosen and arranged on a surface in a layout to grant an idea.

The previous part of Graphic Design was well,Graphic.The part i am now going to explain is the second part of Graphic Design which is Design. Design is to decide on how it is to look and the function of somethimg before it is made.In lament terms Design is

  • Thinking
  • Problem solving
  • Feasibility

In Graphic Design there are rules that can be used.These rules are known as the "Principles Of Design."These Principles have a relationship between each other and appear in every designed piece of work one would see. The Key Principles of design are:

  • Composition
  • Balance
  • Rhythm/Movement
  • Emphasis
  • Contrast
  • Proportion

When we bring Visual/Art elements and the Design Principles togethwer we have GRAPHIC DESIGN.
